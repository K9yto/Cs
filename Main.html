Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro (born February 5, 1985) is a Portuguese footballer, forward, captain of the Saudi club An-Nasr and the Portuguese national team

Iker Casillas Fernandez (born 20 May 1981, Madrid) is a Spanish footballer who played as a goalkeeper. Former captain of the Spanish national team.

Paolo Maldini (born June 26, 1968) is an Italian footballer, long-time captain of Milan and the Italian national team. He played as a central and left back.

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior (born 5 February 1992), known as Neymar Júnior or mononymously as Neymar, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Saudi Pro League club Al Hilal and the Brazil national team. A prolific goalscorer and playmaker renowned for his flamboyant style

Zinedine Yazid Zidane (French: Zinédine Yazid Zidane; born 23 June 1972), popularly known as Zizou, is a French professional football manager and former player who played as an attacking midfielder. He most recently coached Spanish club Real Madrid and is one of the most successful coaches in the world
